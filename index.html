About This Project

This project documents the design and construction of an autonomous LiDAR-based 4WD robot car using a Raspberry Pi 4B, ROS 2 Humble, USB/Arducam camera, and PS4 controller. The goal is to build a portable robotics platform capable of remote control and obstacle-aware navigation.

Team Members: Connor Reagan,Hugo Acevedo,Dhruveen Sheth
