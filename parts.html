Core Processing & Control Units

  • Raspberry Pi 4 Model B – Sensor data processing, motor control, image data
  • Adafruit Motor HAT – Motor driver for independent wheel control

Sensors & Peripherals

  • RPLIDAR A1 – 2D LiDAR for mapping and obstacle detection
  • Logitech Brio 100 Camera – Vision-based navigation
  • Ultrasonic Sensors (optional) – Proximity detection

Power System

  • 18650 Batteries (x2) – Power for motors and HAT
  • Power Bank (optional) – For Raspberry Pi

Mechanical Components

  • Chassis Frame
  • Brushless DC Motors – Efficient and durable
  • Mecanum or Regular Wheels

Storage

  • 64GB microSD Cards – OS and logging

Software & Frameworks

  • Python – Main programming language
  • ROS2 Humble – Real-time mapping and control
  • Custom Python Scripts – Motor control logic

Miscellaneous

  • Breadboard & Jumper Wires – For prototyping
  • Mounts & Brackets – For camera, sensors, and boards
  • Screws, Spacers, Bolts – For structural assembly
